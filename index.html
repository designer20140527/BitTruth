Core Features

Unlocking Transparency in Digital Content

Language Emotion Manipulation Detection

BitTruth's AI technology deeply analyzes advertising copy, social media content, and other text to reveal hidden emotional manipulation tactics (such as fear or urgency), helping you avoid being misled by manipulative marketing.

Micro-Expression and Voice Emotion Analysis

Whether it's an advertisement, a job interview, or a social media video, BitTruth utilizes facial micro-expressions and tone of voice analysis to assess the authenticity of emotions, giving you insight into the true intent behind them and helping you make better decisions.
